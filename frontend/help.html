How to Play

Objective

The goal of the game is to fill the white squares with letters, forming words or phrases, by solving clues which lead to the answers.

Controls

  • Click on a cell to select it.
  • Type a letter to fill in the selected cell. The selection will automatically move to the next cell in the word.
  • Use the arrow keys to navigate the grid.
  • Click on a clue in the list to highlight the corresponding word on the grid.

Scoring

  • You get 10 points for each correctly guessed word.
  • Your total score is the sum of the points for all solved words.

Features

  • Show Answers: If you get stuck, you can use the "Show Answers" button to reveal the correct answers. This will end the current game.
  • Leaderboard: See how you rank against other players on the leaderboard.
